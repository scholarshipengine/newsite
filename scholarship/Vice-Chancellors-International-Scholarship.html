Vice-Chancellor's International Scholarship - Ongoing anually

Welcome to Macquarie University - Where Academic Excellence is Rewarded! The Vice-Chancellor's International Scholarship (VCIS) at Macquarie University is designed to recognize and reward outstanding academic performance, attracting high-achieving students from around the world. This highly competitive scholarship is based on academic merit and is awarded to talented students pursuing a Bachelor's or Master's coursework degree at Macquarie University. With the VCIS, you can receive up to AUD $10,000 applied towards your tuition fee, providing valuable financial support to pursue your academic goals. However, please note that this is a limited scholarship, so we encourage you to accept your offer and scholarship early to secure your spot and avoid disappointment. To be eligible for this prestigious scholarship, you must be a full-time international student commencing an undergraduate or postgraduate coursework degree (excluding Graduate Certificates and Global MBA courses) and meet the following criteria: Achieve a minimum Weighted Average Mark (WAM) equivalent of 65 for Postgraduate applications; or a minimum Australian Tertiary Admission Rank (ATAR) equivalent of 85 for Undergraduate applications. Commence your study in the session and year indicated in your scholarship letter of offer. At Macquarie University, we value academic excellence and are committed to supporting talented students like you. Don't miss this opportunity to be recognized for your achievements and pursue your studies at our esteemed institution. Apply for the VCIS today and embark on an enriching academic journey at Macquarie University!
