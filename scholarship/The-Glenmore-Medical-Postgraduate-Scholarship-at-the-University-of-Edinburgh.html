The Glenmore Medical Postgraduate Scholarship at the University of Edinburgh

About The Glenmore Medical Postgraduate Scholarship at the University of Edinburgh

The University of Edinburgh is pleased to offer scholarships to applicants who have been accepted for admission in the 2023-2024 academic year to one of the eligible Human Medical programmes listed above. To be eligible for consideration, applicants should have already been offered a place at the University of Edinburgh and should have firmly accepted that offer or be intending to do so. Successful scholarship recipients are expected to use their medical education to make a positive impact in countries that are in need of improved healthcare. In order to access the scholarship application system, applicants must have applied for admission to the University of Edinburgh and should complete an online application form. The deadline for scholarship applications is 11:59 BST on June 1, 2023. To apply for this prestigious scholarship, it is important to visit the official website (link found below) for detailed information on the application process and to access the online application form. The University of Edinburgh is committed to supporting talented and dedicated individuals pursuing medical education, and this scholarship presents a unique opportunity to further your studies and make a meaningful contribution to communities in need.
