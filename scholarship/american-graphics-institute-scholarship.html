American Graphics Institute Scholarship, Eligibility

DEADLINE: Closes 31.July/31.Dec.2023

About American Graphics Institute Scholarship, Eligibility

The American Graphic Institute has two scholarships: the Design Thinking scholarship and the Training scholarship. The Design Thinking scholarship is open to part-time and full-time students at accredited US colleges and universities. It awards $1,000 twice a year, in January and August. The scholarship aims to promote design thinking across disciplines. Deadlines for submissions are July 31 and December 31, and submissions are considered for the next available scholarship. To apply, submit a 500-1500-word essay on how design thinking or digital design tools will impact your career.

The Training scholarship is awarded to students enrolled in Certificate programs at American Graphics Institute. It grants $500 to students currently enrolled or scheduled to be enrolled during the scholarship application. To apply, submit a 500-1500-word essay on how learning digital design tools like Photoshop, Illustrator, and InDesign will contribute to your career.

Eligibility Criteria:

  • The Design Thinking Scholarship requires enrollment in a degree-granting program at an accredited two or four-year college or university in the United States. Any accredited college or university qualifies, and it is not limited to students of American Graphics Institute.
  • Only one scholarship application per person is allowed for each scholarship period.
  • The scholarship recipient must either be a legal resident of the United States or possess a valid student visa.
  • A completed scholarship application form must be submitted.
