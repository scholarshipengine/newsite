Concerto Competition for High School Musicians

Concerto Competition for High School Musicians, Application

DEADLINE:Closes 15.Nov.2023

About Concerto Competition for High School Musicians

The President's Own" United States Marine Band, together with the Marine Corps Heritage Foundation, joyfully announces the opening of its Concerto Competition for High School Musicians. Interested participants can now submit their application materials, with the deadline set for Nov. 15, 2023. The fortunate winner of the competition will be featured as a guest soloist alongside the esteemed Marine Chamber Orchestra during the 2024 concert season. Additionally, they will be honored with a well-deserved $2,500 scholarship from the Marine Corps Heritage Foundation. Acknowledging exceptional talent, the second and third-place winners will also be rewarded with scholarships of $1,000 and $500, respectively.

Eligibility Criteria:

  • Open to high school musicians (grades 9-12) enrolled in 2023-2024. Eligible: strings, piano, and harp. (Woodwind, brass, and percussion eligible in 2025.)

Application Materials:

  • Performance Recording: Applicants are required to choose a single piece from the repertoire list and provide an unedited recording of their performance with piano, band, or orchestra accompaniment. Recordings should be submitted as YouTube videos, and the application form necessitates a link to the video. Optionally, applicants can mark the video as "unlisted," ensuring that it remains accessible only to those with the direct link. Additionally, applicants have the choice to not appear in the video if they prefer.
  • Online Application Form: Submit all required information on the online application form.
