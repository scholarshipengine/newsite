DEADLINE:Closes 15.Oct.2023

About The Daniels Scholarship Program, Application, Eligibility, Deadline

The Daniels Scholarship Program offers motivated students the chance to pursue their higher education aspirations. By being selected as Daniels Scholars®, students are granted the opportunity to receive a maximum of $100,000, which can be utilized at any nonprofit, accredited college or university in the United States, regardless of whether it is a two- or four-year institution. The allocation of funds is based on the financial requirements of the individual. We are seeking students who possess exceptional potential, exhibit strong character, and harbor ambitious dreams.

Daniels Scholars represent the upcoming generation of leaders in America, displaying resilience in the face of life's obstacles while upholding the values cherished by Bill Daniels. These individuals possess an independent and innovative mindset, taking pride in their nation and local community, and dedicating themselves to its betterment through hard work. If you believe you fit this description, we encourage you to apply and become part of an extraordinary community comprising nearly 5,000 Daniels Scholars and alumni nationwide.

Eligibility Criteria:

  • Reside in CO, NM, UT, or WY.
  • High school senior ready to graduate.
  • US citizen or permanent resident.
  • Minimum Grade Point Average of 3.0
  • Minimum SAT Math score of 490 and minimum Evidence-Based Reading & Writing score of 490, or minimum ACT score of 18 in each category (excluding writing score).

Financial Eligibility:

  • To qualify for the Daniels Scholarship Program, applicants must show financial need by having an Adjusted Gross Income (AGI) of $100,000 or less on their 2022 federal tax return, considering the applicant's dependency status. An additional $10,000 of income is allowed for each extra dependent claimed on the 2022 tax return.
