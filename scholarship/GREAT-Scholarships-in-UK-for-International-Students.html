GREAT Scholarships in UK for International Students - Closes June.2023

The GREAT Scholarships program provides students from 14 countries with an opportunity to receive £10,000 towards their tuition fees for a wide range of one-year taught postgraduate courses. In the academic year 2023-24, a total of 200 scholarships are offered by 49 universities across England, Wales, Scotland, and Northern Ireland. The GREAT Scholarships are available to students from the following countries: Bangladesh, China, Egypt, Ghana, Kenya, India, Indonesia, Malaysia, Mexico, Nigeria, Pakistan, Thailand, Turkey, and Vietnam. To find out which scholarships are available in your country, you can click on the links provided. Each scholarship is jointly funded by the UK government's GREAT Britain Campaign and the British Council, in collaboration with participating UK higher education institutions. As part of the program, all GREAT scholars will be invited to a welcome event in the first semester and a networking event in the second semester. This initiative aims to promote educational opportunities and strengthen ties between the UK and these countries, providing deserving students with financial support to pursue their postgraduate studies in the United Kingdom.

Financial Support: Scholarships provide financial assistance to cover tuition fees, living expenses, and other educational costs. Access to Opportunities: Scholarships can provide access to various opportunities, including internships, research projects, and study abroad programs, which can help enhance academic and professional growth. Networking: Scholarships can offer networking opportunities with other scholarship recipients, alumni, and donors, providing students with valuable connections in their field of study. Personal Development: Applying for a scholarship can also help students develop important skills, such as writing, research, and presentation skills, which can be useful in their future academic and professional endeavors.
