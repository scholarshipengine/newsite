Nottingham Developing Solutions Scholarships - Closes 17.May.2023

The Developing Solutions scholarship is a prestigious master's scholarship program founded in 2001, offered by the University of Nottingham for students from African, South Asian, and other selected Commonwealth countries. This scholarship is specifically designed for international students who are passionate about making a positive impact on the development of key economic, environmental, structural, social, or political structures in their home country. The University of Nottingham is providing scholarships that cover 50% or 100% of full-time master's tuition fees. These one-year scholarships are aimed at supporting innovative and visionary students who can demonstrate their ingenuity in developing solutions that will create meaningful change in their home countries. Applicants will be primarily assessed based on their vision and tangible plan for addressing a specific economic, environmental, structural, social, or political challenge in their home country, and how this aligns with the learning outcomes of their chosen course of study. Strong applicants will be able to showcase how their efforts will contribute to positive outcomes. In addition to demonstrating their vision, applicants will also be considered based on more traditional scholarship criteria, such as their reasons for choosing the University of Nottingham and noteworthy achievements. The ethos of Developing Solutions, which focuses on creating impactful solutions, will be a key factor in the assessment process.
