About College JumpStart Scholarship

The College JumpStart Scholarship was born from our late-night conversations with a simple question in mind: How can we empower students to achieve their college dreams, We recognized the importance of paying it forward and believed that by initiating this scholarship program, others would be inspired to do the same. Join us in making a difference by supporting students who demonstrate a passion for learning and unwavering perseverance.

Eligibility Criteria:

  • Applicants must currently be enrolled as high school students (10th-12th grade), college students, or have plans to enter college within the next 12 months. U.S
  • >U.S. citizenship or legal residency is a requirement.
  • The online application, which includes a personal statement, must be completed.
